# Initial Concept

Cadenza: A sheet music analysis, rendering, and playback platform.

# Product Definition - Cadenza

## Vision
Cadenza is a specialized sheet music analysis, rendering, and playback platform designed for musicians and music students. It provides a bridge between static musical scores and interactive digital performance tools, enabling deep analysis and high-fidelity practice sessions.

## Target Audience
- **Musicians:** Performers seeking interactive tools to practice and interpret scores.
- **Music Students:** Learners utilizing digital analysis and playback to enhance their music theory and practice.

## Core Goals
- **High-Fidelity Rendering & Playback:** Provide a seamless, interactive experience for viewing and listening to MusicXML and MIDI files.
- **Musical Analysis:** Offer structured insights and visualizations of musical data (MusicXML) to support learning and performance preparation.

## Key Features
- **Score Management:** A centralized dashboard to upload, organize, and manage digital music assets (MusicXML, MIDI).
- **Interactive Music Player:** A synchronized playback engine with a visual cursor, powered by WebMscore and Tone.js, allowing for tempo control and part isolation.
- **User Authentication:** Secure access to personal score libraries via NextAuth.js and PostgreSQL.

## Design & UX Principles
- **Aesthetic:** Professional, clean, and minimalist design that prioritizes score readability.
- **UX:** Intuitive navigation with a focus on a "distraction-free" practice environment.
- **Theme:** High-clarity professional interface with refined typography and subtle, meaningful interactions.
